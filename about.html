Get to know Claudia

I’m Claudia! I’m a web developer and traveller. My journey into tech began with a Bachelor’s Degree in Fashion Business from Fashion Institute of Technology (FIT) in NYC. I took what I learned at the world’s top fashion school to the University of Waterloo where I earned a Master’s Degree in Business, Entrepreneurship and Technology. Today as a developer and graduate of Web Development Bootcamp at Juno College of Technology, I bring everything I love about UI/UX, technology and innovation to every line of code I write.

Coding is about making life better, beautiful, accessible and fun. Take a look at my applications, tell me what you think, and reach out if you want to work together! Welcome to my site.
